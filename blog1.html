OJT Recognition

This photo was taken during the awarding of my OJT certificate at DENR. It marks the completion of my 16-week training, where I gained valuable experience, learned from professionals, and grew both personally and professionally.

This photo captures one of the most memorable highlights of my On-the-Job Training (OJT) at the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR). I was honored to receive a certificate of completion, symbolizing the end of a fulfilling 16-week journey filled with learning, growth, and meaningful experiences. Standing beside me are some of the dedicated and supportive DENR staff who guided and mentored me throughout the program. Their encouragement and hands-on guidance helped me gain valuable insights into environmental management, administrative work, and the real-world applications of my academic knowledge. This moment is not just a token of completion—it’s a reminder of the hard work, collaboration, and personal development I experienced during my time with the DENR.

Advice to Readers

Embrace every learning opportunity during your OJT or internship. Don’t be afraid to ask questions, seek guidance, and step out of your comfort zone. The experiences and connections you make will help shape your future career and personal growth. Always give your best, stay curious, and enjoy the journey!
